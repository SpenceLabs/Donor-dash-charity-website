What We Plan To Do?

Your Donations, Their Futures: Nourishing Lives, Educating Minds, Empowering Dreams

Provide Food and Basic Needs

Setting up weekly food drives. Offering meals at strategic locations where street children gather. Providing water, clothes, and hygiene kits.

Clean Drinking Water

We will provide safe and pure drinking water to children in need, protecting them from waterborne diseases and ensuring they stay healthy and hydrated.

Healthcare for Street Children

We will organize periodic health camps to screen for common issues such as malnutrition, respiratory infections, and skin conditions.

School Enrollment and Supplies:

Donations will be used to enroll children in local schools and provide them with uniforms, school supplies, and other essential learning materials such as textbooks, notebooks, and stationery.

Safe Shelters

A safe place to sleep and call home is essential for every child. We will work to provide secure residential facilities that offer not just shelter but a sense of stability and belonging.

Psychosocial Support

Beyond physical needs, emotional and social care are critical for these vulnerable children. Our programs will offer counseling, mentorship, and social support, helping children build self-esteem and resilience with your help
